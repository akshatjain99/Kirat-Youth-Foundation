Our Origin Story

Kirat Youth Foundation is a club of young Delhiites who have come together to bridge the gap between community and health. Observing the poor health system,we have teamed up to affect the health status of the different sections of the population.

Vision

The KYF team pledges to help the civilised and uncivilised people by holding awareness talks about a person’s well being, campaigns to motivate people to take better steps for their fitness and many more such activities focussing on numerous aspects of public and personal health and hygiene.

Projects

RISE

The initiative “RISE” i.e "Responsibility to Inform about Safe Eradication" includes working for the improvement of health facets like nutrition, vaccination, disease free conditions etc as well as recognizing contraception as an essential method of population control.

Sort. Shine. Sustain

“SORT” stands for removing the unnecessary items and disposing them properly. “SHINE” stands for cleaning the working area and “SUSTAIN” means to continue the practice. With the motive of “Neat around, clean surround should be our keen behind”, this project focuses on maintenance of public cleanliness and sanitation.

iClean

Promoting the importance of personal health and hygiene amongst the masses with provision of the best possible ways to maintain personal cleanliness, the organization has come up with the project “iClean”.

Internships

The Kirat Youth Foundation presents an opportunity of internship in the field of your choice in this organization. The time period of internship is 4-6 months and one can work in any of the following departments as per his/her choice:

  1. Content & Research Development
  2. Photography/Videography
  3. Operations
  4. Social Media & Publicity
