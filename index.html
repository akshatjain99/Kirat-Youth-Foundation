About Us

Who we are

OUR ORIGIN STORY :

KYF, Kirat Youth Foundation, is a club of young Delhiites who have come together to bridge the gap between community and health. Observing the poor health system, few individuals decided to take a righteous step and work for the welfare of the people in this sector. With wide range of ideas, they teamed up to affect the health status of the different sections of the population in rural and urban areas.

MISSION AND VISION :

With the motto of bridging the gap between the community and health, this youth club is a non-governmental organization working towards the betterment of health of the masses by carrying out drives or campaigns focussing on numerous aspects of public and personal health and hygiene. The KYF team pledges to help the civilised and uncivilised people by holding awareness talks about a person’s well being, campaigns to motivate people to take better steps for their fitness, cleanliness drives and many more such activities.

What We Do

iClean

Promoting the importance of personal health and hygiene amongst the masses with provision of the best possible ways to maintain personal cleanliness, The Kirat Youth Foundation has come up with the project “iClean”. The programme encompasses various aspects of individual health, ranging from oral hygiene to hand sanitation, nail and skin health, safe drinking water etc. and taking necessary measures to spread awareness about it.

RISE

Abiding by our motto of bridging the gap between health and the community, Kirat Youth Foundation has started an initiative “RISE” i.e "Responsibility to Inform about Safe Eradication" which includes working for the improvement of health facets like nutrition, vaccination, disease free conditions etc as well as recognizing contraception as an essential method of population control.

Sort. Shine. Sustain.

“Sort. Shine. Sustain” which drives people’s attention towards the maintenance of public cleanliness and sanitation. Here, “SORT” stands for removing the unnecessary items and disposing them properly. “SHINE” stands for cleaning the working area and “SUSTAIN” means to continue the practice. With the motive of “Neat around, clean surround should be our keen behind”, we pledge to make the environmental conditions better for ourselves and for others by undertaking activities like proper disposal of garbage, spreading awareness about usage of public toilets etc.

Social Media & Publicity TO INTERN WITH US, MAIL US AT kiratyouthfoundation@gmail.com

Volunteer

Our team invites all the people who wish to make a difference in the society by giving their contribution for the welfare of fellow citizens. You can register yourself as a volunteer in any part of the country and choose the project of your choice you wish to work in. A volunteer who takes part in at least 4 drives will be provided with a certificate and Letter Of Recommendation will be given to a volunteer who participates in at least 10 drives. We welcome the involvement of people of all age groups.
