About Us

Who we are

Our Origin Story :

KYF, Kirat Youth Foundation, is a club of young Delhiites who have come together to bridge the gap between community and health. Observing the poor health system, few individuals decided to take a righteous step and work for the welfare of the people in this sector. With wide range of ideas, they teamed up to affect the health status of the different sections of the population in rural and urban areas.

Mission and Vision :

With the motto of bridging the gap between the community and health, this youth club is a non-governmental organization working towards the betterment of health of the masses by carrying out drives or campaigns focussing on numerous aspects of public and personal health and hygiene. The KYF team pledges to help the civilised and uncivilised people by holding awareness talks about a person’s well being, campaigns to motivate people to take better steps for their fitness, cleanliness drives and many more such activities.

What We Do

iClean

Promoting the importance of personal health and hygiene amongst the masses with provision of the best possible ways to maintain personal cleanliness, The Kirat Youth Foundation has come up with the project “iClean”.

Sort. Shine. Sustain

Here, “SORT” stands for removing the unnecessary items and disposing them properly. “SHINE” stands for cleaning the working area and “SUSTAIN” means to continue the practice.With the motive of “Neat around, clean surround should be our keen behind”,

RISE

“RISE” i.e "Responsibility to Inform about Safe Eradication" which includes working for the improvement of health facets like nutrition, vaccination, disease free conditions etc as well as recognizing contraception as an essential method of population control.
